About Us – CareVet Animal Hospital

Welcome to Happy Tails Veterinary Clinic, where your pet's health and happiness come first. Founded by Dr.Omkar Raut a compassionate and experienced veterinary professional, our clinic is dedicated to providing high-quality medical care with a personal touch.

At our clinic, we believe that pets are more than animals — they are family. That’s why we offer a wide range of veterinary services tailored to your pet's individual needs. From preventive care and vaccinations to surgery and emergency treatment, we are here for your furry companions every step of the way.

With a clean, comfortable environment, modern equipment, and a caring team, we strive to make every visit stress-free for both pets and their owners. Whether you're coming in for a routine check-up or a more serious concern, you can count on us to treat your pet with kindness, patience, and expertise.
